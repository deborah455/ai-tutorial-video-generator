How the pipeline works

  1. 1 The video is processed frame-by-frame using OpenCV and FFmpeg.
  2. 2 OCR extracts on-screen text to identify key actions and UI changes.
  3. 3 The recording is segmented into logical steps and converted into clear, human-readable instructions.
  4. 4 A text-to-speech engine generates narration aligned with the detected steps.
  5. 5 FFmpeg rebuilds the final tutorial video with the original visuals and the generated voiceover.

Designed for training and product walkthroughs

This interface demonstrates an automated approach to converting product flows and internal workflows into repeatable, narrated guides. It’s ideal for onboarding, customer education, feature launches, and internal process documentation.
